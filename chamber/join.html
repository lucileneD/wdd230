Join Newnan Chamber

Benefits Reserved for Members

NP Membership


$0/year

This tier caters specifically to non-profit organizations and comes at no cost. It provides equivalent benefits to those of a Silver 'Member'.

Bronze Membership


$200/year

You'll receive access to quarterly mixers, professional development workshops, and a 15% discount on contracted business insurance premiums through our exclusive insurance partner

Silver Membership


$300/year

You'll receive access to all features included in the Bronze membership, along with a 25% discount on ad space in a leading local newspaper, a 30% discount on insurance premiums through our partner, and direct communication with our chamber's board of legal advisors.

Gold Membership


$400/year

You'll gain access to all the perks of the Silver membership, and on top of that, you'll enjoy quarterly marketing opportunities at local grocery stores, VIP reserved tents at Newnan farmer's markets, featured space on our town chamber's website, priority registration for chamber events, personalized business development consultations, exclusive access to industry reports and market insights, discounted rates on advertising and promotional services, and invitations to special networking gatherings.
