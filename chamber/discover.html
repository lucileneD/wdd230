Activities to Explore in Newnan and the Surrounding Area



Walking Dead Tour

Embark on an exclusive journey behind the curtains of the acclaimed television series, 'The Walking Dead,' with our expert-guided walking tour. Delve into the atmospheric Esco Feed Mill property, a backdrop for numerous pivotal scenes in the show. As part of the adventure, select a replica prop weapon to accompany your stroll, mirroring the iconic choices seen in the series. Your knowledgeable guide will lead you through the significant locations, providing an exhilarating and immersive experience.

Newnan Development:


Newnan is a charming city located in Coweta County, Georgia, United States. Situated approximately 40 miles southwest of Atlanta, Newnan is known for its rich history, Southern hospitality, and a blend of modern amenities with a small-town feel. The city has experienced notable growth in recent years while maintaining its historic character and sense of community.

Stone Mountain Park

Simplify your trip to Stone Mountain with a half-day small-group tour. Discover the natural history of this massive granite mountain at various attractions within Stone Mountain Park. Hike nature trails, visit the Stone Mountain Museum, and ride the cable car to the summit for breathtaking views of Georgia's landscape. Enjoy ample free time for your own exploration.


Stranger Things tour

Maximize your exploration of Stranger Things filming locations in less time by joining this guided tour. Your knowledgeable guide is well-versed in efficient routes throughout Atlanta and the surrounding area, ensuring you cover more ground than if you were to explore independently. Enjoy the convenience of hotel pickup and drop-off, traveling in comfort. Throughout the journey, your guide will provide behind-the-scenes insights, enhancing your experience with fascinating information about key scenes from the show.


Dunaway Gardens

Established in 1934 as an original venue for theatrical training and home to some of the nation's most cherished stage performances, Dunaway stands out as a unique rock and floral garden, showcasing unparalleled beauty across all seasons. Ideal for weddings, this location is steeped in history, featuring enchanting walking paths and breathtaking scenery at every turn. Whether you're celebrating a wedding or simply enjoying a day out, Dunaway is a splendid destination with its rich history and picturesque surroundings. It's undeniably a cool spot worth experiencing.


Ashley Park

Ashley Park stands as an open-air,mixed-use shopping center, presenting a diverse range of gourmet dining and trendy shopping options. It serves as an ideal destination for a romantic date night with your spouse or a fun-filled girls' night out with your best friends!


Carl Miller Park

This park is owned by Newnan Utilities but is open to the public and enjoys support from numerous local families and businesses. In addition to standard playground equipment, you'll find custom-made wooden structures like "castles," "school buses," "airplanes," and more, encouraging imaginative play for visitors of all ages.


World of Coca-Cola

Visit World of Coca-Cola in Atlanta with this skip-the-line admission ticket to the home of the famous beverage brand. See the vault that holds the secret Coca-Cola recipe, and browse exhibits with more than 1,200 Coca-Cola artifacts. Then watch a 3-D movie that spans the globe and sample more than 100 different beverages.
