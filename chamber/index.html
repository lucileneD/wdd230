Welcome to the Newnan Chamber of Commerce


Our Purpose


Our organization is dedicated to championing the success of our members and the broader community. We act as a driving force in cultivating a thriving business environment through our commitment to business-friendly public policies, support for diverse and innovative business initiatives, and the provision of valuable programs and services to our members. At the Newnan Chamber of Commerce, we are the bridge where business and community intersect. Members have the unique opportunity to connect with fellow members, fostering a more robust and supportive business climate.
