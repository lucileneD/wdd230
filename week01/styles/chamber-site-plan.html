Newnan Chamber of Commerce- Site Plan

Site Purpose

The primary objective of our website is to champion the prosperity of our members and the community at large, serving as the nexus where business and community interests converge. It facilitates connections among members, fostering a robust business environment. Whether one is eager to explore the enchanting locales of our town, delve into entrepreneurial pursuits, seek investment opportunities, expand an existing business, or engage in networking, the Newnan Chamber of Commerce website is a comprehensive platform offering a wealth of resources, services, and connections to support diverse goals.

Target Audience

Newnan Chamber of Commerce website is tailored to a diverse audience with a keen interest in the economic landscape, business prospects, and community involvement within the Coweta County area.

Personas

Jessica Hammond - Psychologist:

Meet Jessica Hammond, a 43-year-old Brazilian Psychologist who recently relocated to Newnan, GA. With over a decade of residence in the USA, Jessica envisions establishing her own clinic and seeks guidance, resources, and support to navigate the local business milieu. She is eager to connect with seasoned professionals in her area, gaining insights into local regulations and licensing procedures.

Daniela Adami - Local Entrepreneur:

At just 18 years old, Daniela Adami embarked on a journey of entrepreneurship, crafting premium hand-spun cotton candy from her unique recipes since the age of 14. As her small business burgeons, Daniela is now strategically evaluating potential locations, market opportunities, and potential business partnerships. She actively seeks networking opportunities with clients and is keen on attending business events and workshops to enhance her skills and knowledge.

Betty Yeaman - Retired nurse:

Meet Betty Yeaman, a 67-year-old retired nurse, divorced with four children and seven grandchildren. Embracing retirement, Betty is unwavering in her commitment to learning, serving, and growing within the community. She is particularly interested in women's networking, dedicating her newfound time to community service and volunteering at various events.

Scenarios

1. Jessica's Journey into Entrepreneurship: Jessica utilizes the website to connect with experienced professionals, access resources, and gather insights into local business regulations as she embarks on the journey of establishing her own psychology clinic.

2. Daniela's Business Expansion: Daniela navigates the website to explore potential business locations, identify market opportunities, and connect with potential partners and clients to further expand her successful hand-spun cotton candy business.

3. Betty's Post-Retirement Engagement: Betty engages with the community through the website, seeking women's networking opportunities, community service initiatives, and events where she can volunteer her time and expertise, contributing to the betterment of the community.

Color Scheme

The predominant color for this website is green, chosen for its association with nature, mirroring the lush surroundings of Newnan, adorned with splendid trees. Green, being a color symbolizing hope, has been deliberately selected to convey optimism. To maintain a clean aesthetic, a lighter shade of green complements the dominant color, with a background rendered in a pristine white.
